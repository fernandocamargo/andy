About me

Welcome to andysparkles, where passion meets adventure and style! Since its inception in 2013, my blog has been fueled by my deep-rooted interests in writing, fashion, and photography. As my digital footprint expanded to Instagram in 2014, so did my community, steadily growing in size and engagement. Today, I work as both a seasoned blogger and a professional photographer, where maintaining high-quality standards is non-negotiable.

With a focus on fashion, lifestyle, and beauty, I strive to curate content that resonates with my audience, offering them a glimpse into my vibrant world. In 2019, I embarked on an exciting journey as a solo female traveler, sharing my experiences and insights with fellow wanderers.

While my blog remains a cornerstone of my digital presence, Instagram serves as my primary channel, boasting a dedicated following of over 70k enthusiasts. With 90% of my blog audience based in Germany and 60% on Instagram, I am deeply connected to my local and international community.

In addition to creating captivating narratives, I collaborate with clients to craft compelling videos, blog posts, user-generated content (UGC), and offer comprehensive social media management services, ensuring their brand message resonates effectively with their target audience.

Join me as we explore the convergence of travel, fashion, and lifestyle, one captivating story at a time. Welcome to andysparkles, where every moment is an adventure waiting to unfold.
